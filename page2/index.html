Here we go!


10 August 2015

So, I’ve read several discussions about how I really should be recording things I do and things I learn in a journal to show as proof that I’m actually doing and learning things. That sounded like good advice, and so I’ve put together this blog. I’m going to record my experiences doing security-related stuff at a large research university. I’ll also document anything else I learn along the way, because learning is good, even if it’s not directly security-related.
