10 August 2015

So, I’ve read several discussions about how I really should be recording things I do and things I learn in a journal to show as proof that I’m actually doing and learning things. That sounded like good advice, and so I’ve put together this blog. I’m going to record my experiences doing security-related stuff at a large research university. It’s a really challenging environment, and I’m very glad that I spent seven years working all across the university in a client-oriented support role before pivoting to Information Security. Without the contacts and context, I’d be completely adrift.
